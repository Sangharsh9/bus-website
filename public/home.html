How it works ?

A valid bus pass is required to travel by the college bus. Please follow the steps mentioned above to issue your bus pass. Without a bus pass, access to the college bus service will not be permitted.

Visit the Stationery Store

Go to the college's stationery store in person. This is where the process to issue your bus pass begins.

Provide Your Details

  • Your name
  • Pick-up or on board location

Price Confirmation

The staff will determine the cost of the bus pass based on your specified location

Make the Payment

Pay the appropriate fee for your monthly two-way bus pass

Collect Your Pass

Once payment is confirmed, you will receive your monthly two-way bus pass, which allows you to travel on the college bus for the designated month.
